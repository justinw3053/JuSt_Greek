# Unit 1: The Greek Alphabet

## Goal
Learn to read, write, and pronounce the 24 letters of the Greek alphabet.

## The Letters (Lower & Upper)
*   **Α α** (Alpha) - Like 'a' in "father".
*   **Β β** (Beta) - Like 'v' in "very" (NOT 'b'!).
*   **Γ γ** (Gamma) - Like 'gh' soft sound (or 'y' before i/e).
*   ... (to be completed with NotebookLM questions)
